About Sentinel Hack

This 24 hour hackathon is an initiative taken by the K.S Institute of Technology, to inspire the students to pursue their passion towards a technical future. The students can make use of this opportunity to improve their knowledge base in various fields and come up with innovative ideas to solve real world problems.



The hackathon focuses on bringing students with similar mind-sets together in order to provide them with a chance to enhance their capabilities and work towards achieving a common goal centred on an intriguing set of different ideas.

FAQs

  • When and Where?

    Sentinel Hack will take place on November 9th and 10th at K.S Instiute of Technology - 14, Kanakapura Main Road, Municipal Corporation Layout, Raghuvanahalli, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560109

  • How much will it cost?

    The participation cost per head is Rs.150.

  • Duration?

    You will have 24 hours to build your products. Prepare to spend 2 days with us starting friday morning up until Saturday afternoon. A more detailed scheduled will be released closer to the event.

  • Food?

    We will provide you with enough free grub to keep you hacking for 24 hours. Also we have stalls placed at your convience .

  • How do teams work?

    Teams can have a minimum of 2 and maximum of 4 members. Teams can also constitute of memebers from different colleges registered together.

  • How does registration work?

    Apply on the google form given. Your payment can be done via paytm and a screenshot of transaction confirmation needs to be uploaded in the given section. There are a limited number of spots available, so Hurry up! You will be recieving a confirmation mail upon finalization.

  • What if this is my first hackathon?

    Awesome! We have fun surprizes for newcomers, as the best way to learn is by building. There will be mentors to help you every step of the way. We all start somewhere, why not here?

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your college ID cards, laptop, chargers and spike busters. That's all from your side !

  • Anything else?

    Shoot us an email or find us on Instagram. We are happy to help!
